What are domains?

Data in the catalogue is organised into data 'domains'. These are a grouping of data by subject area.

When data is related to more than one domain, we have assigned it to the one that appears most relevant.

Domains may be influenced by the organisational structure but they are intended to be narrower in scope than an entire agency.
