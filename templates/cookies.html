Cookies

This site saves small files (known as ‘cookies’) onto your device.

Cookies are used to:

  • remember when you sign into the site
  • measure how you use the site so it can be updated and improved based on your needs

These cookies are not used to identify you personally.

Essential cookies

Name Purpose Expires
sessionid Remembers that you are signed in 2 weeks
csrftoken Protects against unauthorised access 1 year

Analytical cookies

Analytics cookies collect information about how you use this site. This helps us make sure the site is meeting the needs of users and helps us make improvements.

We use Google Analytics to learn about:

  • the pages you visit
  • how long you spend on each page
  • how you got to the site
  • what you click on while you are using the site

We do not collect or store your personal information (for example your name or address) so this information can’t be used to identify who you are. We do not allow third parties to use or share our analytics data.

The site may save the following analytics cookies:

Name Purpose Expires
_ga Used to distinguish users 2 years
_ga_<container-id> Used to persist session state 2 years

You can use a browser addon to opt out of Google Analytics cookies on all websites.
